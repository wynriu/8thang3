# **App Name**: CelebrationFortunes

## Core Features:

- User Personalization Input: Allow users to input their name and birth year to personalize their fortunes.
- AI-Powered Fortune & Wish Generator: A generative AI tool that crafts unique and uplifting fortunes and wishes, intelligently incorporating the user's name and birth year, ideal for celebratory occasions.
- Fortune Display: Present the personalized fortune or wish in an easy-to-read and aesthetically pleasing format.
- Animated Emoji & Heart Effects: Display interactive animations including moving emojis and heart particle effects when a fortune is revealed, enhancing the celebratory mood.
- Sharing Option: Allow users to easily share their personalized fortune or wish on social media platforms or with contacts.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A vibrant and warm rose-pink (#D9268C) to evoke joy and femininity, resonating with the celebratory theme.
- Background Color: A soft, delicate pale pink (#F9EDF5) to maintain a bright, festive atmosphere while ensuring high readability against the primary color.
- Accent Color: A rich and regal magenta (#A317CF) to provide depth and contrast, adding an extra layer of festivity and luxury to key elements.
- Headlines and Body Text: 'Poppins' (sans-serif), a geometric and contemporary font that provides a playful yet sophisticated feel suitable for celebratory messages and clear readability.
- Employ a lively set of emojis and stylized heart icons. Use gold accents for icon details and outlines to enhance the festive, celebratory theme.
- Implement a card-based layout for fortunes, with ample whitespace to ensure focus on content, framed by dynamic visual elements. Elements should flow engagingly, suitable for mobile screens.
- Introduce animated transitions for fortune generation and display. Focus on flowing particle effects (hearts, glitter) and gently moving emojis that create a sense of magic and celebration.
